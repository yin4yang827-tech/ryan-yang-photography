About

Photography is a way of paying attention.

I'm Ryan Yang, a landscape photographer and I love taking photos anywhere I go. I'm drawn to quiet moments — mist rising off a waterfall, sun slipping through a canopy, a city softening at dusk.

My work is made with a Nikon D90. My favorite time to take photos is during sunset, usually after a bit of rain.
